Our mission

GMTC Japanは、中高生のエンジニアリングやコーディングの実践的なプロジェクトに取り組む機会を提供する、学生主導のグループです。 GMTC Japan is a student-led initiative providing middle school and high school students with the opportunity to collaborate on hands-on engineering/coding projects that go beyond what is typically offered in school.
